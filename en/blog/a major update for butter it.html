A Major Update for Butter It!

Date: 02/25/2021

Butter It just got a huge update!


Enjoy melting butter with relaxing background music and the following brand new features:


  • NEW LOGIN SYSTEM. Your data will be saved in the cloud and you will only need your username and a password (assigned by the system) to link your data to all the devices you want. Your data will always stay synchronized in real time between all your devices.
  • NEW VERSION FOR WINDOWS. It doesn't matter if your computer doesn't have a microphone, you can use the space bar!
  • YOUR SCORE IS NOW MARKED. The leaderboard now marks your own score in yellow so you don't lose sight of it.
  • IMPROVED OPTIMIZATION. If the game was at 5fps it will now run at 6fps.
  • IMPROVED GRAPHICS. You may not even notice it.


I've also worked restless to solve the following bugs:


  • It was unfairly difficult to get past pan 14.
  • Problems with the 19:9 aspect ratio have been corrected.
  • Some notifications used to come out incomplete and some other overflowing the text box.
  • And something else I can't remember.


If you find some other bugs, use the 'Report Bug' button from the main menu.


I'm also proud to announce... THE SPECIAL VERSION!

If you donate $1USD+ on Itch.io you'll receive a Special Bundle, which includes:


  • Interface customization. Color the game interface the way you like.
  • Personalization of the frying pan. Choose the color of the pan before starting to play.
  • Stage customization. Choose between the 3 scenarios offered by the game.
  • Your name will be marked with a star that will show everyone that you have the special version.
  • More goodbye and reminder notifications.
  • All my love and gratitude.


All this in Butter It: Fixed 3.0.0!


Thanks for your attention.
