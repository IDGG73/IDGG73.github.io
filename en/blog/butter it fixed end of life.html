Butter It: Fixed - End of Life

Date: 08/25/2021

In september 12th 2020, Butter It was released. A few days after that, it was updated and renamed to Butter It: Fixed.


Today, almost one year after, Butter It will be out.

The reason for this is how outdated and inefficient my code was, causing a lot of heat and lag in a wide variety of devices. While it was playable, it requiered a ridiculous amount of power considering its simplicity in both gameplay and graphics.


Another reason for this, is Dreamlo. I decided to use Dreamlo to store the data of all accounts, as it had a limit of 100 users, which was a pretty acceptable limit for my game and all the data from the server was accessible using simple HTTP requests.

The problem is that Dreamlo lowered the limit to only 25 users for a free leaderboard, which is too low even for a game like Butter It and I don't have any money.


So, what will happen to Butter It?


Butter It: Fixed will be replaced with Butter It Deluxe, which is already in the making and is a new Butter It made entirely from scratch that will have new features, like a scene editor.

THE OLD BUTTER IT: FIXED WILL BE REMOVED FROM DOWNLOAD. All the special functions from the original game will come back and be included in the base game for free*. In fact, Butter It Deluxe will not have any special version nor ads.


I'm still thinking what to do about the leaderboard, one solution I can think of, is sending the user record through a Google Form and manually upload it to a list hosted in GitHub which can be read by the game. Pros: I can verify the legitimacy of the records myself and discard any suspicious record easily. Cons: I have to verify every record manually.

This solution seems pretty good. There won't be a huge amount of players at first, so it's fine and easy for me to keep track of records. If for some reason Butter It becomes popular and a lot of people starts playing it will be a PAIN, but luckily I'd have the money to pay for a Dreamlo-like solution.

But right now, the leaderboard has some high chances of being scrapped.


THANKS FOR PLAYING BUTTER IT: FIXED!


* Excluding the "Special" message and star from the leaderboard, if any.
